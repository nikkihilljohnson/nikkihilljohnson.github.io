Objective

Build brand awareness and drive qualified leads through organic social and Meta ad campaigns in a low-interest category. The challenge: homeowners don't think about HVAC until it breaks — making it essential to reach them long before they need to make a purchase.

The Approach

  • Built full-funnel paid & organic social strategy from scratch
  • Defined HVAC.com's brand voice and paid media foundation from the ground up
  • Led with empathy and education — reaching homeowners before their system breaks
  • Awareness campaigns building trust through relatable, problem-solving content
  • Retargeting to nurture ready-to-buy audiences into qualified leads
  • Aligned organic storytelling with performance-driven Meta campaigns

The Insight That Drove Everything

Homeowners are deeply emotionally invested in their homes—but completely disengaged from HVAC equipment. The unlock was making the content about the feeling of comfort, safety, and a healthy home environment, not the hardware.

Content Strategy

  • Editorial calendar built around seasonal pain points (winter prep, summer heat, allergy season)
  • Short-form video Reels showing relatable homeowner moments + expert tips
  • Meme-driven content to humanize the brand and drive organic shares
  • Educational carousel posts on air quality, filter maintenance, and energy savings
  • Reddit-style testimonials and social proof to build credibility

Paid Media Strategy

  • Meta campaigns structured across awareness, consideration, and conversion objectives
  • Ongoing A/B testing of creative formats, hooks, and audience segments
  • Retargeting audiences built from website visitors, video viewers, and engaged followers
  • CPL and ROAS tracked weekly, optimized monthly based on cohort data

Results Breakdown

  • ~$58K budget → 1.35M+ impressions at 2.97% CTR
  • 423 qualified leads at $71 Cost Per Lead
  • Meta campaigns directly attributed to ~6% of total company sales
  • Social audience grew 4,000% through organic content strategy
  • 83% increase in video views across platforms
  • 231K+ total engagements driven across organic and paid

Objective

The Rebrand Strategy
